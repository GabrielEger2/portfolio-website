High School

Coming from a low-income family, I never had the opportunity to study at the best private Brazilian schools, meaning I was stuck in the terrible management and lack of resources of the public schools; This all changed in 2017 when I decided to take a competitive test to an IF (Instituto Federal) close to my home.
